Welcome and thank you for visiting

The Expat pages

This website is still very much under construction but we chose to make it live in the hopes that visitors such as yourself might already find some use of the info contained within.

We hope that The Expat Pages will become a resource of information for potential expats who might be considering packing up their bags making the move to Mexico, in particular, Cancun. Furthermore, and perhaps even more importantly, it’s a pool of shared knowledge for those of us who are already here surviving and thriving in this 3rd world paradise.

Please do bear in mind that this website is a labour of love and as such might take a while before it becomes fully operational. However if you would like to help by sharing your experiences with us, I would love to hear from you. At the moment I am especially interested in how parents in the expat community have gone about choosing schools or home schooling for their kids – in particular, what unexpected difficulties did you (and your children) face and what do you wish you knew before you started?
